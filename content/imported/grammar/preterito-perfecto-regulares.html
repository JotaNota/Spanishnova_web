Present Perfect Regular Verbs in Spanish

Let's form sentences like this:

  • Yo he comido - I have eaten

Se forma con haber (presente) + participio regular.

  • haber
    • Yo he
    • Tú has
    • él / ella ha
    • Nosotros hemos
    • Ellos han
    • Ustedes han

Participio:

  • Ending ar → -ado
    Hablar → Hablado
  • Ending er → -ido
    comer → comido.
  • Ending ir → -ido
    vivir → vivido

El pretérito perfecto tense describes:

  • Recently completed actions
    → He terminado mi tarea. I have finished my homework.
  • Life experiences (without saying when)
    → He visitado México. I have visited Mexico.
  • Actions within a time period that is still ongoing (today, this week, this year)
    → Hoy he estudiado mucho. Today I have studied a lot.
  • Past actions with a result or relevance in the present
    → He perdido mis llaves. I have lost my keys.

Common time expressions used with this tense
→ hoy, esta semana, este mes, ya, todavía, nunca, alguna vez.

Conjugación de verbos regulares

Oraciones afirmativas

  • Yo he trabajado mucho - I have worked a lot.
  • Tú has cocinado pizza -You have cooked pizza.
  • Ella ha estudiado inglés - She has studied English.
  • Nosotros hemos limpiado la casa -We have cleaned the house.
  • Ellos han visitado a sus abuelos - They have visited their grandparents.

Oraciones negativas

  • Yo no he estudiado hoy -I have not studied today.
  • Tú no has cocinado nada - You have not cooked anything.
  • Él no ha viajado este mes - He has not traveled this month.
  • Nosotros no hemos escuchado la canción - We have not listened to the song.
  • Ellos no han jugado todavía -They have not played yet.

Oraciones interrogativas

Yes/No:

  • ¿Has trabajado hoy? Have you worked today?
  • ¿Ha cocinado ella? Has she cooked?
  • ¿Hemos hablado ya? Have we talked already?
  • ¿Han estudiado ustedes? Have you (plural) studied?
  • ¿Ha cantado bien ella? Has she sung well?

WH-questions:

  • ¿Qué has cocinado? What have you cooked?
  • ¿Dónde has trabajado? Where have you worked?
  • ¿Cuándo hemos comido? When have we eaten?
  • ¿Con quién ha vivido ella? Who has she lived with?
  • ¿Por qué han limpiado? Why have they cleaned?
