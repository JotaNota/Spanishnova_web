# Reading Structure Variants

Use this file to select the content skeleton for readings.

`default_variant` comes from `docs/content-system/roadmaps/reading-roadmap.csv`.

Do not print `default_variant` in the lesson body.

## `narrative_profile`

Use for biographies, historical figures, discoveries, and stories about one person or group.

Skeleton:

```txt
# Title

Brief English intro.

## Vocabulario

6-8 key terms.

## Section 1

Two short paragraphs.

## Section 2

Two short paragraphs.

## Section 3

Two short paragraphs.

## Section 4

Two short paragraphs.

## Hoy / Cierre

Optional short closing paragraph.

## Ideas clave

5 bullets.

## Actividad

### Verdadero o falso

5 statements with answers.

### Sobre la lectura

4 comprehension questions.

### Para ti

2 personal questions.
```

Rules:
- Use section headings that move the story forward.
- Do not create headings for isolated explanatory questions.
- Keep section rhythm consistent.
- Each paragraph should have two to four sentences.

## `practical_situation`

Use for daily-life readings, travel scenes, work situations, food places, neighborhoods, and simple personal experiences.

Skeleton:

```txt
# Title

Brief English intro.

## Vocabulario

6-8 useful words or phrases.

## Section 1

One or two short paragraphs.

## Section 2

One or two short paragraphs.

## Section 3

One or two short paragraphs.

## Section 4

Optional one or two short paragraphs.

## Ideas clave

4-5 bullets.

## Actividad

### Verdadero o falso

5 statements with answers.

### Sobre la lectura

4 comprehension questions.

### Para ti

2 personal questions.
```

Rules:
- Keep the language concrete and useful.
- Include useful phrases inside the scene.
- Do not add disconnected grammar notes.

## `procedural_set`

Use for recipes, routines, instructions, comparisons of similar processes, and grouped how-to lessons.

Skeleton:

```txt
# Title

Brief English intro.

## Vocabulario

Key ingredients, materials, or action words.

## Item 1

### Qué lleva / Qué necesitas

Short list.

### Preparación / Proceso

Short ordered steps.

## Item 2

### Qué lleva / Qué necesitas

Short list.

### Preparación / Proceso

Short ordered steps.

## Item 3

### Qué lleva / Qué necesitas

Short list.

### Preparación / Proceso

Short ordered steps.

## Item 4

Optional grouped item.

## Verbos útiles

Use only when repeated actions are central.

## Ideas clave

4-5 bullets.

## Actividad

### Traducir

8-12 items.

### Preguntas

6-8 questions.

### Adivina

Optional guessing activity for grouped items.
```

Rules:
- Keep each grouped item parallel in length and shape.
- Use the same subheadings across grouped items.
- Do not let one item become much longer than the others.

## `curiosity_article`

Use for animal facts, culture notes, science curiosities, expressions, and informative articles.

Skeleton:

```txt
# Title

Brief English intro.

## Vocabulario

8-10 key terms.

## Section 1

One or two short paragraphs.

## Section 2

One or two short paragraphs.

## Section 3

One or two short paragraphs.

## Section 4

One or two short paragraphs.

## Section 5

Optional one or two short paragraphs.

## Ideas clave

5-7 bullets.

## Actividad

### Preguntas de vocabulario

4-5 questions.

### Sobre la lectura

6-10 questions.

### Para ti

2 personal questions.
```

Rules:
- Do not repeat headings.
- Do not include an index inside the lesson body.
- Use bullets only for lists of facts or examples.
- Clean duplicated titles before generating Markdown.
