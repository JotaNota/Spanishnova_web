# Music Vocabulary in Spanish

Learn Spanish music vocabulary for talking about songs, instruments, concerts, and favorite artists.

## Vocabulary

### Music Basics

- **la m?sica** -> music
- **la canci?n** -> song
- **el ritmo** -> rhythm
- **la letra** -> lyrics
- **el sonido** -> sound
- **el volumen** -> volume

### People and Instruments

- **el cantante** -> singer
- **la banda** -> band
- **el m?sico** -> musician
- **la guitarra** -> guitar
- **el piano** -> piano
- **la bater?a** -> drums

## Common Phrases

- **Me encanta esta canci?n.** -> I love this song.
- **La letra es muy bonita.** -> The lyrics are very beautiful.
- **Toco la guitarra.** -> I play guitar.
- **Sube el volumen, por favor.** -> Turn up the volume, please.

## Fun Phrase

**Canto mejor cuando nadie me escucha.** -> I sing better when nobody hears me.

## Exercises

### Translate

1. I love this song.

   Me encanta esta canci?n.

2. The lyrics are very beautiful.

### Complete
