# Symptoms Vocabulary in Spanish

Learn Spanish symptom vocabulary for explaining how you feel at a clinic, pharmacy, or doctor appointment.

## Vocabulary

### Common Symptoms

- **el dolor** -> pain
- **la fiebre** -> fever
- **la tos** -> cough
- **el resfriado** -> cold
- **el mareo** -> dizziness
- **la n?usea** -> nausea

### Body Areas

- **la cabeza** -> head
- **la garganta** -> throat
- **el est?mago** -> stomach
- **la espalda** -> back
- **el pecho** -> chest
- **la pierna** -> leg

## Common Phrases

- **Me duele la cabeza.** -> My head hurts.
- **Tengo fiebre.** -> I have a fever.
- **Me siento mareado.** -> I feel dizzy.
- **Necesito ver a un doctor.** -> I need to see a doctor.

## Fun Phrase

**Mi cuerpo dice que hoy toca descansar.** -> My body says today is for resting.

## Exercises

### Translate

1. My head hurts.

   Me duele la cabeza.

2. I have a fever.

   Tengo fiebre.

### Complete

1. Me _____ siento mareado.

   Me siento mareado.

2. Me _____ duele la cabeza.

   Me duele la cabeza.
