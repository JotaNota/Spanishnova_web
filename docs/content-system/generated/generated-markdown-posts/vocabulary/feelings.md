# Feelings Vocabulary in Spanish

Learn Spanish words for feelings and emotions so you can describe your mood and understand others.

## Vocabulary

### Basic Feelings

- **feliz** -> happy
- **triste** -> sad
- **enojado** -> angry
- **nervioso** -> nervous
- **tranquilo** -> calm
- **cansado** -> tired

### More Emotions

- **preocupado** -> worried
- **orgulloso** -> proud
- **sorprendido** -> surprised
- **aburrido** -> bored
- **confundido** -> confused

## Common Phrases

- **Estoy feliz hoy.** -> I am happy today.
- **Me siento un poco nervioso.** -> I feel a little nervous.
- **Ella est? preocupada.** -> She is worried.
- **Necesito estar tranquilo.** -> I need to be calm.

## Fun Phrase

**Tengo muchas emociones y solo una taza de caf?.** -> I have many emotions and only one cup of coffee.

## Exercises

### Translate

1. I am happy today.

   Estoy feliz hoy.

2. I feel a little nervous.

   Me siento un poco nervioso.

### Complete

1. Ella _____ est? preocupada.

   Ella est? preocupada.

2. Estoy _____ feliz hoy.

   Estoy feliz hoy.
