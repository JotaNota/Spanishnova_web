Learn Spanish vocabulary for daily routines, morning habits, and everyday activities.

Vocabulary

Morning

  • despertarse → to wake up
  • levantarse → to get up
  • ducharse → to shower
  • vestirse → to get dressed
  • desayunar → to have breakfast

Day and Night

  • trabajar → to work
  • estudiar → to study
  • almorzar → to have lunch
  • cenar → to have dinner
  • acostarse → to go to bed

Common Phrases

  • Me despierto a las siete. → I wake up at seven.
  • Desayuno caf? y pan. → I have coffee and bread for breakfast.
  • Trabajo por la tarde. → I work in the afternoon.
  • Por la noche descanso. → At night I rest.

Fun Phrase

Mi rutina empieza mejor con caf?. → My routine starts better with coffee.
