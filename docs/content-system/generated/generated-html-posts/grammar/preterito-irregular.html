Irregular preterite verbs still talk about completed past actions, but their stems and endings do not follow the regular pattern. Learn the most common forms as practical chunks.

Overview

Many high-frequency verbs are irregular in the preterite: tener, estar, poder, poner, saber, hacer, querer, venir, decir, traer, ser, ir, dar, and ver. The good news is that several of them share the same endings.

  • Ayer tuve una reunión importante. → Yesterday I had an important meeting.
  • Ellos fueron al centro después del trabajo. → They went downtown after work.

Conjugación

Subject Forma Example Translation
Yo tuve / estuve / hice / fui Yo tuve tiempo ayer. I had time yesterday.
Tú tuviste / estuviste / hiciste / fuiste Tú hiciste la reserva. You made the reservation.
Él / Ella / Usted tuvo / estuvo / hizo / fue Ella fue a la oficina. She went to the office.
Nosotros / Nosotras tuvimos / estuvimos / hicimos / fuimos Nosotros estuvimos en casa. We were at home.
Ellos / Ellas / Ustedes tuvieron / estuvieron / hicieron / fueron Ellos hicieron la comida. They made the food.

Cómo/cuándo lo usamos

Very common finished actions

Use irregular preterite forms for completed actions with common verbs like tener, hacer, estar, ser, and ir.

  • Tuve una idea. → I had an idea.
  • Fuimos al supermercado. → We went to the supermarket.

Shared irregular endings

Many irregular preterite verbs use endings like -e, -iste, -o, -imos, -ieron, without accent marks.

  • Pudimos terminar temprano. → We were able to finish early.
  • Ustedes pusieron la mesa. → You all set the table.

Ser and ir look the same

Ser and ir have the same preterite forms. Context tells you whether fue means was or went.

  • La reunión fue corta. → The meeting was short.
  • Ana fue al banco. → Ana went to the bank.

Oraciones.

Afirmativas

  • Yo tuve una buena semana. → I had a good week.
  • Tú hiciste el plan. → You made the plan.
  • Ella puso las llaves en la mesa. → She put the keys on the table.
  • Nosotros fuimos al parque. → We went to the park.

Negativas

  • No pude llamar antes. → I could not call earlier.
  • Él no quiso salir. → He did not want to go out.
  • Nosotras no estuvimos en la reunión. → We were not at the meeting.
  • Ellos no dijeron nada. → They did not say anything.

Forma de pregunta

  • ¿Tuviste tiempo ayer? → Did you have time yesterday?
  • ¿Ella hizo la reserva? → Did she make the reservation?
  • ¿Fuimos al lugar correcto? → Did we go to the right place?
  • ¿Ustedes trajeron los documentos? → Did you all bring the documents?
