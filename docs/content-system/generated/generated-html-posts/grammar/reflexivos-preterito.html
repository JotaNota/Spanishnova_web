Reflexive verbs in the preterite describe completed actions where the subject also receives the action. The verb moves to the past, but the reflexive pronoun still matches the subject.

Overview

Use me, te, se, nos, and se before the preterite verb: me levanté, te acostaste, se duchó, nos vestimos, se fueron. The pronoun does not change because of the tense.

  • Me levanté temprano ayer. → I got up early yesterday.
  • Ellos se quedaron en casa anoche. → They stayed home last night.

Cómo/cuándo lo usamos

Daily routine that happened once

Use the preterite when a routine action happened at a specific time or as one finished event.

  • Me duché después del gimnasio. → I showered after the gym.
  • Se despertaron a las seis. → They woke up at six.

Completed change of state

Reflexive preterite forms can show that someone became, got, or ended up in a state.

  • Me sentí mejor después de comer. → I felt better after eating.
  • Ella se puso nerviosa. → She got nervous.

Movement and staying

Common verbs like irse and quedarse are reflexive in meaning and often appear in past stories.

  • Nos fuimos temprano. → We left early.
  • Se quedaron en el hotel. → They stayed at the hotel.

Estructura

Patrón Example Translation
reflexive pronoun + preterite verb Me desperté a las siete. I woke up at seven.
no + reflexive pronoun + preterite verb No nos acostamos tarde. We did not go to bed late.
question + reflexive pronoun + preterite verb ¿Te bañaste después de correr? Did you bathe after running?
plural subject + se + preterite verb Ellas se vistieron rápido. They got dressed quickly.

Oraciones.

Afirmativas

  • Me levanté a las ocho. → I got up at eight.
  • Tú te acostaste temprano. → You went to bed early.
  • Ella se vistió para la reunión. → She got dressed for the meeting.
  • Nosotros nos fuimos después de cenar. → We left after dinner.

Negativas

  • No me desperté con la alarma. → I did not wake up with the alarm.
  • Él no se bañó por la mañana. → He did not bathe in the morning.
  • Nosotras no nos quedamos mucho tiempo. → We did not stay for a long time.
  • Ellos no se sintieron bien. → They did not feel well.

Forma de pregunta

  • ¿Te levantaste temprano? → Did you get up early?
  • ¿Ana se puso nerviosa? → Did Ana get nervous?
  • ¿Nos quedamos aquí? → Did we stay here?
  • ¿Ustedes se fueron tarde? → Did you all leave late?
