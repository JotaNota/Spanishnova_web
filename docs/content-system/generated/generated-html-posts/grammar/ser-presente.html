The verb ser is essential in Spanish to express identity, traits, and professions. Let’s explore how to use it correctly and practice with examples.

Overview

Verb “Ser” — How Do We Use It?

Let’s express an idea with this verb:

Yo soy Steve Rogers → I am Steve Rogers

We already have the first use: expressing names.

But Spanish verbs are conjugated, so let’s look at its present tense forms.

Structure

Structure Example Translation
Subject + ser + nameYo soy Steve Rogers.I am Steve Rogers.
Subject + ser + nationalityTú eres uruguayo.You are Uruguayan.
Subject + ser + professionTú eres estudiante.You are a student.
Subject + ser + relationshipEllos son amigos.They are friends.
Subject + ser + descriptionElla es alta.She is tall.

Negations and Yes / No Questions

Negations

Add “no” before the verb:

  • Ella no es Verónica. - She is not Verónica.

Yes / No Questions

Just change the intonation or use question marks:

  • ¿Ella es Verónica? - Is she Verónica?

Uses

Names

Use ser to identify people by name.

  • Él es Steve Rogers. - He is Steve Rogers.
  • Ella es Wanda Maximoff. - She is Wanda Maximoff.
  • Él es Tony Stark. - He is Tony Stark.
  • Ella es Natalia. - She is Natalia.

Nationalities

Use ser to talk about origin or nationality.

  • Él es mexicano. - He is Mexican.
  • Ella es canadiense. - She is Canadian.
  • Él es japonés. - He is Japanese.
  • Ella es brasileña. - She is Brazilian.

Professions

Use ser to say what someone does or is professionally.

  • Él es médico. - He is a doctor.
  • Ella es ingeniera. - She is an engineer.
  • Él es profesor. - He is a teacher.
  • Ella es cantante. - She is a singer.

Relationships

Use ser to express family or social relationships.

  • Él es mi hermano. - He is my brother.
  • Ella es mi hermana. - She is my sister.
  • Él es mi enemigo. - He is my enemy.
  • Ella es mi madre. - She is my mother.

Physical Descriptions and Personality

Use ser to express traits and descriptions.

  • Él es alto. - He is tall.
  • Yo soy baja. - I am short.
  • Él es fuerte. - He is strong.
  • Ella es inteligente. - She is intelligent.
