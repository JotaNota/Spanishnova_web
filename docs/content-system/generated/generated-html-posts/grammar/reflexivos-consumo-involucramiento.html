Spanish sometimes uses reflexive verbs to show personal involvement, completion, or enjoyment. This is common with verbs like comer, beber, comprar, leer, and ver.

Overview

In sentences like me comí la pizza or me compré una chaqueta, the reflexive pronoun does not mean myself. It shows that the action matters personally to the subject or that the subject fully consumed, used, or obtained something.

  • Me comí toda la pizza. → I ate the whole pizza.
  • Ella se compró una chaqueta. → She bought herself a jacket.

Cómo/cuándo lo usamos

Completed consumption

Use the reflexive pronoun when the whole item is consumed or finished.

  • Me bebí el agua. → I drank the water up.
  • Ellos se comieron todos los tacos. → They ate all the tacos.

Personal benefit

With comprar and similar verbs, the reflexive pronoun can show that the subject got something for themselves.

  • Tú te compraste zapatos nuevos. → You bought yourself new shoes.
  • Nos compramos boletos para el concierto. → We bought ourselves tickets for the concert.

Strong involvement

The reflexive pronoun can make the action feel more complete, personal, or involved.

  • Me vi toda la serie. → I watched the whole series.
  • Ella se leyó el informe completo. → She read the complete report.

Estructura

Patrón Example Translation
reflexive pronoun + consumption verb + object Nos bebimos el café. We drank the coffee up.
reflexive pronoun + comprar + object Me compré un libro. I bought myself a book.
reflexive pronoun + leer / ver + whole object Se leyó la novela. He or she read the whole novel.

Oraciones.

Afirmativas

  • Me comí la sopa. → I ate up the soup.
  • Tú te compraste una camisa. → You bought yourself a shirt.
  • Ella se leyó el libro. → She read the book all the way through.
  • Nosotros nos bebimos el café. → We drank the coffee up.

Negativas

  • No me comí el postre. → I did not eat the dessert.
  • Él no se compró nada. → He did not buy himself anything.
  • Nosotras no nos vimos la película. → We did not watch the movie.
  • Ellos no se bebieron el jugo. → They did not drink the juice up.

Forma de pregunta

  • ¿Te comiste todo? → Did you eat everything?
  • ¿Ella se compró una bicicleta? → Did she buy herself a bicycle?
  • ¿Nos leímos el capítulo? → Did we read the chapter all the way through?
  • ¿Ustedes se bebieron el té? → Did you all drink the tea up?
