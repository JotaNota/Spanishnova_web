Overview

Regular commands help you tell someone what to do in a clear, practical way. The form changes depending on whether you speak to t?, usted, ustedes, or nosotros. Focus on the message first, then choose the form that fits the context.

  • Regular imperative: ejemplo principal. → Regular imperative: main example.
