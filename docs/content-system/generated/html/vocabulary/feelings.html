Learn Spanish words for feelings and emotions so you can describe your mood and understand others.

Vocabulary

Basic Feelings

  • feliz → happy
  • triste → sad
  • enojado → angry
  • nervioso → nervous
  • tranquilo → calm
  • cansado → tired

More Emotions

  • preocupado → worried
  • orgulloso → proud
  • sorprendido → surprised
  • aburrido → bored
  • confundido → confused

Common Phrases

  • Estoy feliz hoy. → I am happy today.
  • Me siento un poco nervioso. → I feel a little nervous.
  • Ella est? preocupada. → She is worried.
  • Necesito estar tranquilo. → I need to be calm.

Fun Phrase

Tengo muchas emociones y solo una taza de caf?. → I have many emotions and only one cup of coffee.
