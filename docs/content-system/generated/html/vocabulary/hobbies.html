Learn Spanish hobby vocabulary for talking about free time, interests, and favorite activities.

Vocabulary

Creative Hobbies

  • leer → to read
  • dibujar → to draw
  • pintar → to paint
  • cocinar → to cook
  • escribir → to write
  • tocar un instrumento → to play an instrument

Active and Relaxing Hobbies

  • bailar → to dance
  • correr → to run
  • nadar → to swim
  • ver pel?culas → to watch movies
  • escuchar m?sica → to listen to music
  • jugar videojuegos → to play video games

Common Phrases

  • Mi pasatiempo favorito es leer. → My favorite hobby is reading.
  • Me gusta cocinar los fines de semana. → I like to cook on weekends.
  • Quiero aprender a pintar. → I want to learn to paint.
  • Escucho m?sica para relajarme. → I listen to music to relax.

Fun Phrase

Tengo demasiados pasatiempos y muy poco tiempo libre. → I have too many hobbies and too little free time.
