The preterite talks about completed actions in the past. Use it when an action has a clear beginning, end, or finished result.

## Overview

The Spanish preterite is common with yesterday, last night, last week, specific dates, and actions that happened once or were completed.

- Ayer estudié español. -> Yesterday I studied Spanish.
- Ella compró café esta mañana. -> She bought coffee this morning.

## Conjugación

| Subject | Forma | Example | Translation |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Yo | -é / -í | Yo hablé con Ana. | I spoke with Ana. |
| Tú | -aste / -iste | Tú comiste temprano. | You ate early. |
| Él / Ella / Usted | -ó / -ió | Ella vivió en Madrid. | She lived in Madrid. |
| Nosotros / Nosotras | -amos / -imos | Nosotros trabajamos ayer. | We worked yesterday. |
| Ellos / Ellas / Ustedes | -aron / -ieron | Ellos salieron tarde. | They left late. |

## Cómo/cuándo lo usamos

### Completed actions

Use the preterite when the action is finished.

- Terminé la tarea. -> I finished the homework.
- Ustedes visitaron el museo. -> You all visited the museum.

### Specific time in the past

The preterite often appears with specific past-time expressions.

- El lunes compré un libro. -> On Monday I bought a book.
- Anoche vimos una película. -> Last night we watched a movie.

### Sequence of events

Use the preterite to tell what happened next in a past story.

- Entré, saludé y me senté. -> I entered, said hello, and sat down.
- Ella llegó y llamó a su mamá. -> She arrived and called her mom.

## Oraciones.

### Afirmativas

- Yo estudié anoche. -> I studied last night.
- Tú escribiste un mensaje. -> You wrote a message.
- Ella abrió la puerta. -> She opened the door.
- Nosotros viajamos a México. -> We traveled to Mexico.

### Negativas

- No terminé el proyecto. -> I did not finish the project.
- Él no comió desayuno. -> He did not eat breakfast.
- Nosotras no salimos ayer. -> We did not go out yesterday.
- Ellos no llamaron después. -> They did not call later.

### Forma de pregunta

- ¿Estudiaste ayer? -> Did you study yesterday?
- ¿Ella compró el café? -> Did she buy the coffee?
- ¿Nosotros llegamos tarde? -> Did we arrive late?
- ¿Ustedes vieron la película? -> Did you all see the movie?
